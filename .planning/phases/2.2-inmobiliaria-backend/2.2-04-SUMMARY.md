- **AgencyMemberGuard at class level**: Replaced manual `resolveAgencyId` pattern that depended on AgencyService. The guard sets `request.agencyId` which `@CurrentAgency('agencyId')` extracts. Cleaner, consistent with other inmobiliaria modules.
- **@IsEmail on candidateEmail**: Plan specified @IsString, but email validation is more appropriate (Rule 2 - missing validation).
- **@IsInt on riskScore**: Plan specified @IsNumber, but risk scores are integers (0-100), @IsInt is more precise.
- **Stats before :id route**: GET /stats defined before GET /:id to prevent "stats" being parsed as UUID.
